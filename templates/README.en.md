# Templates pack — ready to use

*[Version française](README.md)*

Templates to copy into your Obsidian vault. Extracted from the [Vault Methodology Template](../vault-methodology-template.en.md) (v4.1).

## Contents

- `adr-template.en.md` — a decision in ADR format (**the "why"**). Copy into `wiki/decisions/`.
- `context/` — the 4 context notes to fill in first:
  - `ligne-rouge.en.md` — **start with this one**, everything else flows from it
  - `identity.en.md` — who you are, your mission, your target audience
  - `scope.en.md` — focus, sub-domains, out of scope, the 500-page ceiling
  - `naming-conventions.en.md` — closed tag list + naming rules

## Getting-started order

1. Fill in `context/ligne-rouge.en.md` (30 min, **before** any structure)
2. Fill in the 3 other context notes
3. Use `adr-template.en.md` as soon as your first structuring decision comes up — see a filled-in example in [`examples/`](../examples/decision-choisir-nommage-descriptif-adr.en.md)

Full methodology: [vault-methodology-template.en.md](../vault-methodology-template.en.md).
